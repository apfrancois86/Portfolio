About Me

Hello! My name is Alex Francois and I was born and raised in Oakland, California. In 2005 I moved up to Eugene, Oregon to attend school at the University of Oregon. In 2009 I graduated with a degree in accounting and moved back home. I worked in the accounting industry for 6 years. Although I learned a lot and meet some great people during this time I decided that accounting was not what I wanted to do long-term for my career. I want to have a career that is creative, forward thinking, and have more of a focus on problem-solving, logic, and technology. After about a year of soul searching, talking to people in different industries, and doing extensive online reasearh I decided a career in software engineering was right for me. Now I am currently attending Epicodus to build my skill-set and start my career in software engineering. I am extremely excited about this opportunity to re-invent myself!
